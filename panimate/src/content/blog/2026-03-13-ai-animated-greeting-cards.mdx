## What Are AI Animated Greeting Cards?

AI animated greeting cards combine the personal touch of a handwritten message with the magic of animation. You provide the words (or let AI help), and technology brings them to life.

### Why They're Better Than Regular Cards

- **Personalized** - Your message, your words
- **Memorable** - Animation captures attention
- **Shareable** - Easy to send via text, email, or social media
- **Professional quality** - Looks like it took hours to make

## Use Cases for AI Greeting Cards

### Birthday Messages

Forget generic "Happy Birthday" texts. Send an animated card with your voice or message that family and friends will actually remember.

### Holiday Greetings

Stand out during holidays when everyone sends the same mass-produced cards. Your animated creation shows you cared enough to make something special.

### Thank You Notes

Make gratitude memorable. An animated thank you shows appreciation in a way a text never could.

### Business Occasions

Corporate birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays - impress clients and colleagues with creative, personalized animations.
